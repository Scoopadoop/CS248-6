Overview

May 15-30, 2015 in Shanghai, Hangzhou & Beijing China

Dive into an intensive study of multicultural and global network design in China! Learn to apply multinational business skills in our changing global economy as you explore the Chinese language and culture in Shanghai, Hangzhou and Beijing. Practice applying international business skills while you work on a team network design case study with IT students from Hangzhou International Studies Universities. Complete your cultural experience with visits to historical sites such as Shanghai-the Bund, Oriental Pearl TV Tower, the West Lake, Forbidden City and, of course, the Great Wall.

Estimated Cost

Billable Expenses $4400 includes airfare, some meals, cultural activities, accommodation, in-country transportation, tuition, OIE service & emergency fee and international health insurance. The exact program fee will be finalized shortly after the application deadline and billed to students by UW-Stout.

Personal Expenses $1150 includes most meals, OIE application fee, passport fees, student visa and spending money.
